Technical Overview

Visit capture workflow from scheduling to verified completion

This page explains how this area of True Care System supports provider operations, EVV readiness, data quality, security, and scalable SaaS delivery.

Schedule Assignment

The workflow begins with structured scheduling so the visit has the correct individual, service, employee, date and planned units.

  • Assigned shift appears in the mobile app
  • Service and authorization context are available
  • Office and field teams share one workflow
  • Schedule data becomes the foundation for EVV validation

Mobile GPS Check-In

DSPs capture the beginning of service directly in the mobile app with time and GPS coordinates.

  • Date and time captured automatically
  • Latitude and longitude recorded
  • Employee and individual are tied to the visit
  • Call assignment supports Time In

Daily Notes and Outcomes

The visit record includes narrative documentation, activities, observations, supports delivered and outcome tracking.

  • Structured service notes
  • Outcome progress monitoring
  • Care team visibility
  • Documentation linked to the visit

Signature and Check-Out

Electronic signature and GPS check-out complete the visit and support billing readiness.

  • Signature timestamp stored
  • Check-out GPS captured
  • Actual duration calculated
  • Visit becomes ready for review
