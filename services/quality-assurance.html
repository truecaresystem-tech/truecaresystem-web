Quality review details

Document review findings and follow-up actions clearly

A strong quality program depends on clear documentation. True Care System helps teams record what was reviewed, what was met, what needs improvement, and what corrective actions must be completed.

  • Track review type, reviewer, review date, and quality score
  • Use checklist-style criteria for care planning and documentation
  • Record strengths, opportunities, and notes
  • Attach supporting documents and review summaries
  • Monitor corrective actions and completion history

Quality visibility from dashboard to mobile

Supervisors and leadership need fast visibility into quality status. With real-time dashboards and mobile-friendly summaries, teams can see overdue reviews, action items, quality scores, and compliance alerts before issues grow.

  • Monitor total reviews, completed reviews, in-progress reviews, and overdue items
  • Track quality score trends across time
  • Review alerts for action items, care plan updates, and training needs
  • Follow documentation and performance indicators in one view
  • Support continuous improvement across multiple providers and programs
