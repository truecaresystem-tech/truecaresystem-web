Operational visibility

See incident activity before it becomes a larger risk

A provider’s ability to respond quickly depends on visibility. True Care System helps leadership and office teams see open incidents, high-priority items, unresolved actions, and recurring trends across care operations.

  • Incident dashboard with total, open, high-priority, and resolved counts
  • Risk-level breakdown for high, medium, and low incidents
  • Recent incident list with status and follow-up visibility
  • Trend monitoring to identify repeated patterns over time
  • Mobile-friendly visibility for field and office coordination

Track the full incident lifecycle

Incident follow-up requires more than a note. Your team needs to know what happened, who reviewed it, what risks were identified, what actions were assigned, and whether those actions were completed on time.

  • Capture incident type, date, location, affected individuals, and description
  • Document risk assessment and potential impact
  • Assign investigation responsibilities and due dates
  • Track corrective action status until completion
  • Support reporting for internal quality and compliance review
