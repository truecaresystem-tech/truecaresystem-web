Authorization visibility

Know what is approved, what is used, and what needs attention

Authorization management is critical for service continuity and billing accuracy. True Care System helps providers avoid service gaps, over-utilization, and missing authorization documentation.

  • Track active, pending, expiring, and expired authorizations
  • Monitor approved hours versus used hours
  • View remaining authorization balances by service
  • Identify expiring authorizations before they disrupt care
  • Support billing readiness with complete authorization records

Providers need visibility before authorization problems affect care delivery. With connected utilization tracking and alerts, office teams can respond earlier and keep services aligned with approved limits.

  • Review expiring authorizations and pending approvals
  • Identify over-utilized services and remaining balances
  • Support supervisors with clearer service control visibility
