Visit workflow

Manage visits from anywhere

Care teams need quick access to visit details before they arrive, during the visit, and after the service is completed. The mobile app keeps essential information close to the person providing care.

  • View visit time, location, service type, and assigned care team
  • Access individual details, service plan items, and important alerts
  • Start visits, add notes, upload documentation, and capture key actions
  • Review attachments, care plans, and required visit information
  • Support clearer communication between mobile staff and office teams
