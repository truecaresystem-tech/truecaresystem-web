Purpose

The BSS module supports case management, assessments, documentation, daily service records, supervisor review, billing readiness, functional behavior assessment, and monthly progress reporting.

HIPAA, Clinical Governance, and Proprietary Training Notice

BSS records may contain highly sensitive behavioral, clinical, family, safety, assessment, incident, and service information. Access must follow the Provider's HIPAA policies, role-based access controls, minimum necessary standard, and applicable professional requirements.

To protect Individuals, Provider-specific clinical procedures, and the proprietary workflow of True Care System, selected advanced BSS functions are intentionally not described in full within this public documentation.

Official Provider customers receive direct role-based training for licensed or otherwise qualified BSS professionals, supervisors, BCBA personnel, and other staff formally authorized by the Provider.

Professional Responsibility

True Care System supports documentation and operational workflows. Behavioral assessment, clinical interpretation, treatment recommendations, crisis planning, supervision, and approval must be completed only by personnel working within their authorized role, credentials, and Provider policies.

General BSS Workflow

STEP 01Create the BSS Case

Add the approved Individual to the Provider's BSS caseload.

STEP 02Complete the Case Record

Review profile, face sheet, required documents, and case assignment.

STEP 03Complete Assessments

Document target behaviors and authorized assessment information.

STEP 04Track Services and Progress

Record daily services, supervisor review, billing readiness, and monthly progress.

Restricted Clinical and Operational Detail

The public documentation does not publish the complete decision process for behavioral assessment interpretation, behavior-function determination, clinical hypothesis, crisis response, restrictive intervention review, supervision standards, billing validation, correction workflows, or Provider-specific escalation procedures.

These workflows are reserved for direct implementation and training sessions with official Provider customers and their authorized BSS professionals.

Direct Provider Training Scope

  • BSS case setup, assignment, level, status, and review cycle
  • Profile, face sheet, and required document completion
  • Problem behavior definitions, severity, risk, and priority
  • MAS and FAST administration and documentation
  • Functional Behavior Assessment workflow
  • ABC data, observation, hypothesis, and function summary
  • BSP implementation and fidelity review
  • Daily billable and non-billable service documentation
  • Supervisor review, corrections, approval, and locking
  • Billing readiness and approved-unit workflows
  • Monthly progress reporting and recommendation review
  • HIPAA, access control, audit logs, and secure document handling

Security and HIPAA

  • Access BSS records only for an approved clinical or operational purpose.
  • Verify the correct Provider, Individual, and BSS case before viewing or entering information.
  • Use the minimum necessary information for the assigned task.
  • Do not share behavioral, clinical, incident, assessment, or document screenshots through unapproved channels.
  • Restrict assessment interpretation and clinical recommendations to properly authorized personnel.
  • Protect uploaded documents, signatures, family information, safety alerts, and case notes.
  • Record access, changes, approvals, deletions, exports, and administrative actions through Audit Logs where applicable.
