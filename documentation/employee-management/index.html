New Employee

The New Employee page is used to create a complete employee profile.

New Employee Sections

Section What Users Enter or Review
Demographics First name, middle name, last name, date of birth, gender, phone, email, education or qualification, and SSN information.
Employment Info Employee ID, role or position, status, hire date, termination date, employment type, branch, primary work location, and supervisor.
Address Home address, city, state, and ZIP code.
Emergency Contact Emergency contact name, relationship, phone, email, preferred language, and address.
Work Preferences Preferred shift, maximum weekly hours, weekend or holiday availability, and additional staffing notes.
Notification Preferences Email, SMS, in-app notification channels and notification topics.
Mobile User Controls whether the employee may access the True Care System mobile application.
