Service Status

Available Status

Status Description
Active Available for authorizations, scheduling, visits, Mobile EVV, billing, payroll, and reporting.
Inactive Not available for new operational activities. Historical records remain available.
Pending The service has been created but is not yet ready for operational use. It cannot be used for authorizations, scheduling, visits, EVV, billing, or payroll until activated.

Active Service

  • Individual service setup
  • Authorizations
  • Scheduling
  • Mobile EVV
  • Visit Maintenance
  • Billing and Payroll
  • Reports

Pending Service

A Pending service is still being configured or reviewed before becoming operational.

  • Cannot be scheduled.
  • Cannot be assigned to authorizations.
  • Cannot be used for visits or billing.
  • Can be activated after configuration is complete.

Inactive Service

  • Cannot normally be selected for new schedules.
  • Cannot normally be assigned to new authorizations.
  • Historical records remain available.
  • Available for reports and audits.
