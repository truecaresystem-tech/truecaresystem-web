Service Status

Version

Status

Last Updated

Purpose

Service Status controls whether a service is available for new operational use in True Care System. Inactive services remain available for historical records, reports, billing history, and audit review.

Available Status

StatusDescription
ActiveAvailable for authorizations, scheduling, visits, EVV, billing, payroll, and reporting.
InactiveNot available for new operational activity. Historical records remain available.

Active Service

An Active service may be used for:

  • Individual service setup
  • Authorizations
  • Scheduling
  • Mobile EVV
  • Visit Maintenance
  • Billing and payroll
  • Reports

Inactive Service

  • Cannot normally be selected for new schedules.
  • Cannot normally be assigned to new authorizations.
  • Remains available in historical visits and billing records.
  • Remains available for reports and audits.

Before Changing Status

  • Active authorizations
  • Future schedules
  • Open visits
  • Pending billing records
  • Individuals currently receiving the service

Best Practices

  • Use Inactive instead of deleting a used service.
  • Do not inactivate a service required by future schedules.
  • Review dependent modules before changing status.
  • Document important status changes when required.

Security and HIPAA

Only authorized users should change Service Status. Status changes should be recorded in Audit Logs.

Although the status field does not contain PHI, changes may affect services associated with individuals. Access should follow the minimum necessary principle.

Troubleshooting

The service does not appear in Scheduling.

Confirm that the service status is Active.

The service cannot be selected in Authorization.

Confirm that the service is Active and correctly configured.

Historical records are missing.

Do not delete services that have historical activity. Change them to Inactive instead.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Inactive remove historical visits?

No. Historical visits, billing records, payroll information, and reports remain available.

Should an unused service be deleted?

Normally, no. Change the service to Inactive.

Can an Inactive service be activated again?

Yes. An authorized user may reactivate it after reviewing the configuration.
