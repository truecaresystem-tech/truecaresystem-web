Purpose

Orders is the master medication profile used to create and maintain scheduled and PRN physician medication orders.

HIPAA, Clinical Governance, and Proprietary Training Notice

Medication information contains highly sensitive protected health information and must be handled under the Provider's HIPAA policies, role-based access rules, minimum necessary standard, and applicable clinical requirements.

To protect medication safety procedures and the proprietary workflow of True Care System, selected advanced functions are intentionally not published in full. Official Provider customers receive direct, role-based training from the True Care System team for licensed nurses and other personnel who are formally authorized by the Provider to manage or administer medications.

Orders Workspace

Standard Order Workflow

1Select the Individual

Verify the correct Individual and reporting month.

2Review Existing Orders

Confirm medication, route, type, schedule, status, and effective dates.

3Add or Edit an Order

Enter medication identity, administration instructions, prescriber, and pharmacy information.

4Validate Clinical Information

Review dose, frequency, start date, end date, allergy flag, and directions.

5Save and Review

Confirm the saved order appears correctly in the master medication profile.

Direct Training Scope

Direct Provider training covers order lifecycle management, physician-order verification, corrections, discontinuation, PRN safeguards, medication-change workflows, and role-specific approvals.
