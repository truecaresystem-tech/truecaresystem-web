Purpose

PRN & Vitals supports documentation of as-needed medication administration, response, notes, and vital signs.

HIPAA, Clinical Governance, and Proprietary Training Notice

Medication information contains highly sensitive protected health information and must be handled under the Provider's HIPAA policies, role-based access rules, minimum necessary standard, and applicable clinical requirements.

To protect medication safety procedures and the proprietary workflow of True Care System, selected advanced functions are intentionally not published in full. Official Provider customers receive direct, role-based training from the True Care System team for licensed nurses and other personnel who are formally authorized by the Provider to manage or administer medications.

PRN Workspace

PRN Documentation Flow

Before

Assess and Verify

Verify the active PRN order, reason, dose, route, timing, and required pre-administration assessment.

During

Administer and Record

Record date, time, medication, dose, route, reason, and necessary notes.

After

Evaluate Response

Document effectiveness, result, response, vital signs when required, and any follow-up.

PRN Fields

FieldGeneral Purpose
Date and TimeRecords when the PRN medication was administered.
MedicationIdentifies the medication from the active order.
Dose and RouteRecords the administered quantity and method.
ReasonDocuments the symptom or approved indication.
Result / ResponseDocuments effectiveness and observed outcome.
NotesStores objective supporting information.

Direct Training Scope

Direct training covers PRN decision criteria, assessment requirements, repeat-dose controls, effectiveness review, vital-sign thresholds, escalation, physician notification, and emergency response.
