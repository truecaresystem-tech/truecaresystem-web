Purpose

Inventory & Controlled tracks on-hand medication and authorized receive, dispense, waste, and adjustment transactions.

HIPAA, Clinical Governance, and Proprietary Training Notice

Medication information contains highly sensitive protected health information and must be handled under the Provider's HIPAA policies, role-based access rules, minimum necessary standard, and applicable clinical requirements.

To protect medication safety procedures and the proprietary workflow of True Care System, selected advanced functions are intentionally not published in full. Official Provider customers receive direct, role-based training from the True Care System team for licensed nurses and other personnel who are formally authorized by the Provider to manage or administer medications.

Inventory Workspace

Transaction Ledger

Receive

Adds medication received from an approved source.

Dispense

Removes medication from inventory for authorized administration or use.

Waste

Records medication destroyed or discarded under approved procedures.

Adjust

Corrects quantity after approved reconciliation or investigation.

Controlled Medication Safeguards

Restricted Workflow

Controlled-substance counts, witness requirements, discrepancy investigation, waste verification, reconciliation, and audit response are intentionally not published in full.

Direct Training Scope

Provider training covers transaction authorization, receiving, dispensing, waste, adjustments, controlled-substance reconciliation, witnessing, discrepancy escalation, exports, and audit preparation.
