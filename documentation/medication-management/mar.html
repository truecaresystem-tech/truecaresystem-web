Purpose

MAR provides the monthly medication administration record for scheduled medications and saved administration outcomes.

HIPAA, Clinical Governance, and Proprietary Training Notice

Medication information contains highly sensitive protected health information and must be handled under the Provider's HIPAA policies, role-based access rules, minimum necessary standard, and applicable clinical requirements.

To protect medication safety procedures and the proprietary workflow of True Care System, selected advanced functions are intentionally not published in full. Official Provider customers receive direct, role-based training from the True Care System team for licensed nurses and other personnel who are formally authorized by the Provider to manage or administer medications.

MAR Status Dashboard

Given

Missed

The scheduled dose was not administered.

Refused

Held

The dose was withheld under an approved direction or condition.

Late

The dose was administered outside the expected time window.

Error

A medication administration exception or error was recorded.

Scheduled

The administration time is upcoming or has not yet been recorded.

Monthly Review Checklist

Restricted Clinical Procedures

The public guide does not publish the complete workflow for missed doses, refusals, held medications, late administration, medication errors, corrections, clinical escalation, or after-hours notification.

Direct Training Scope

Official Provider customers receive direct MAR training for licensed nurses and authorized medication personnel, including exception handling, correction controls, follow-up, audit review, and documentation standards.
