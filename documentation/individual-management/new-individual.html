New Individual

The New Individual page is the starting point for creating a new Individual record. Complete the six setup tabs in sequence before creating the Individual record.

Six Setup Tabs

Tab Description Documentation
1. Profile & Contacts Basic demographic information, contact details, services and emergency contacts. Open guide
2. Coverage & Billing Insurance, payer, eligibility, billing address and guardian/payee information. Open guide
3. Clinical & Medication Physician, medications, allergies and diagnoses. Open guide
4. Preparedness & Equipment Priority level, mobility and medical equipment. Open guide
5. Preferences & Directives Scheduling preferences, language, directives and caregiver preferences. Open guide
6. ISP & BSP ISP goals, BSP, outcomes, authorized services and behavior supports. Open guide
