Purpose

The Individual module is used to create, search, review, and maintain the complete operational record for each person receiving services. This documentation follows the actual True Care System menu structure and the screens currently available to users.

Individual Menu

The Individual menu contains three primary options. These are the main entry points users should reference when working with Individual records.

Menu Purpose Documentation
New Individual Create a new Individual record using the six setup tabs. Open guide
Search Individual Find existing Individuals by code, name, city, county, or status. Open guide
Individual Detail Review and maintain the full record after an Individual has been created. Open guide

New Individual

New Individual uses a six-tab workflow. Users may move between tabs while entering information, save progress as a draft, restore a saved draft, or create the Individual after required information has been completed.

New Individual Tabs

Tab What Users Enter or Review Documentation
1. Profile & Contacts Name, date of birth, gender, Medicare and Medicaid identifiers, branch, primary location, phone numbers, email, address, accepted services, and emergency contact information. Open guide
2. Coverage & Billing Primary and secondary insurance, eligibility status, member or policy ID, group number, coverage dates, billing address, and guardian or representative payee. Open guide
3. Clinical & Medication Primary care physician, medications, allergies, reactions, and diagnosis information. Open guide
4. Preparedness & Equipment Priority code, mobility status, medical equipment, mobility devices, and other dependencies needed for emergency or continuity planning. Open guide
5. Preferences & Directives Scheduling preferences, preferred days and time window, languages, caregiver gender preference, advanced directives, status, physician, and attachments or notes. Open guide
6. ISP & BSP ISP plan status, service coordinator, outcomes, authorized services, health and safety information, BSP behaviors, triggers, prevention strategies, interventions, data collection, responsible team, and attachments. Open guide
Important

The ISP & BSP section may require the Individual record to be created first. When prompted by the system, save or create the Individual, then reopen the record through Search Individual.

Search Individual

Search Individual is the primary screen for locating an existing Individual before reviewing or editing the record.

Available Search Functions

  • Search by Individual code, name, city, or county.
  • Filter records by Pending, Active, or In-active status.
  • Review the total number of matching records.
  • Sort or review Individual name and demographic columns.
  • Select View / Edit to open the Individual Detail record.
  • Select + New Individual to begin a new record.

Search Results

Search results may include photo, Individual code, name, date of birth, city, county, state, ZIP code, phone number, status, and the View/Edit action.

Actual Workflow

In normal use, staff locate the Individual through Search Individual, select View/Edit, and then use the Individual Detail navigation to maintain the record.

Security and HIPAA

Individual records may contain protected health information and personally identifiable information. Access must be limited to authorized users with a legitimate business need.

  • Use role-based access and the minimum necessary principle.
  • Do not access an Individual record without an approved operational purpose.
  • Do not share screenshots containing Individual information outside approved channels.
  • Record access and changes through Audit Logs where applicable.
  • Verify the correct Provider and Individual before editing information.
