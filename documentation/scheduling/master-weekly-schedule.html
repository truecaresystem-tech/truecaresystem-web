Purpose

Connect recurring Master Schedule templates with generated weekly shifts for a selected Individual.

Provider Training Notice

This guide presents the general purpose and standard user flow of this feature. To protect the proprietary scheduling methodology and operational processes developed by True Care System, selected advanced procedures are intentionally not published in full. Official Provider customers receive direct onboarding and role-based training from the True Care System team after becoming active customers.

Workspace Overview

Standard Scheduling Sequence

1Select the Individual

Confirm the correct Individual, ID, template, and service address.

2Review Master Schedule

Review recurring Sunday-through-Saturday events.

3Save the Template

Save approved service, DSP, day, and time changes.

4Generate the Week

Create weekly shifts from the saved template.

5Validate Shifts

Review service, DSP, address, units, and status.

6Review Conflicts

Use Weekly Detail and Summary & Conflicts.

Visible Functions

FunctionGeneral Use
Individual SelectorOpens the schedule for the correct Individual.
Master ScheduleStores recurring service events.
GenerateCreates weekly shifts from the saved template.
Weekly DetailReviews scheduled and actual units.
Summary & ConflictsReviews overlaps and exceptions.
Payroll & ISPSupports unit and staffing comparison.

Training Scope

Direct Provider training covers schedule-generation strategy, recurring-template governance, staffing recovery, unit reconciliation, and advanced conflict resolution.
